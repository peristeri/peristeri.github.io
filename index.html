Open source development work-flow

Most aspiring developers, the enthusiasm of contributing sometime blinds them to the size and complexity of the task.

I had the responsibility of managing an open source project, and became quickly aware of the common oversights and how following a work-flow can help communicate your intent. I find this to be more important then the killer feature I don't understand what it is suppose to do.

Step 1. Explore the project

I will be the first to admit to jumping into a project without reading the docs or the code. And payed for it, by annoying the project maintainer and frustrating myself.
